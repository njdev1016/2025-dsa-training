## Tips for Using This List:

1. **Don't try to solve all problems** - Select based on your weakness
2. **Quality over quantity** - Understand patterns, not just solutions
3. **Start with NeetCode tagged problems** - These are curated essentials
4. **Use filters on LeetCode** - Filter by difficulty and topic
5. **Time yourself** - Practice under time pressure
6. **Review solutions** - Learn multiple approaches
7. **Revisit hard problems** - Come back after a few days
8. **Track completion** - Use LeetCode's progress tracker
9. **Join discussions** - Learn from others' solutions
10. **Practice daily** - Consistency beats intensity
